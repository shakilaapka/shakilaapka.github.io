Bio

For the past 20 years, I have been a social activist and have been involved with many organizations

Nirbhay Bano Andolan

Gen Secretary: 1993 - Present

Agitation and public mobilisation against corruption and communalism


India Center for Human Rights and Law

Field Co-ordinator: 1996 - 2002

To spread legal awareness among the community and find out issues of the people and prepare material for public interest litigation. And after filing, follow-up on implementation.


Parivartan Shikshan Sanstha

Founder and Managing Trustee: 1997 - Present

Providing pre-primary and primary education for slum children, working on right to education and parents empowerment.


National Alliance for People’s Movement

Member: 2004 - Present

Alliance of people’s movement in the country.


Ghar Banao Ghar Bachao Andolan

Member: 2006 - Present

Working in Mumbai for Right to Housing, particularly in slums.


Lok Rajiniti Manch

Member: 2007 - 2009

Trying to give alternative political platform.


Janata Ka Aaina

Printer and Publisher: 2010 - Present

Cover news and issues ignored by mainstream media about marginalized and low income communities.


Aam Aadmi Party

Maharashtra State Executive Member: 2013 - Present

Aiming for a corruption free India and presenting a credible political alternative for the people.
